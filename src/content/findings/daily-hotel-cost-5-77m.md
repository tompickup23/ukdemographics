The National Audit Office confirmed the Home Office was spending an average of **£5.77 million per day** on asylum hotel accommodation (2024/25 financial year average).

**£2.1 billion per year.** On hotels.

30,657 people housed in hotel accommodation as at 31 December 2025 - that's 28.7% of all asylum support.

Meanwhile, 64,426 people are still waiting for an initial decision on their asylum claim. The backlog is the cost driver. Every day of delay is another day of hotel bills charged to YOU.

The 10-year asylum accommodation contract is forecast at **£15.3 billion**.
